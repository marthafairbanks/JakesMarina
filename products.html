Jake’s Marina is the only stop you’ll need to make while visiting Brookville Lake! Our convenient shop has all the provisions needed to explore the region's natural beauty, including boat sales, fishing licenses, bait, sunscreen, ice, snacks and more! Plus we rent bikes, fishing equipment, and kayaks. Call us at (765)-458-6431 for current rates and availability.

Bayliner is the world's largest manufacturer of recreational boats. Established in 1957 by Orin Edson, Bayliner currently has over 400 dealers in over 60 countries around the world.

STINGRAY Boats has been building boats for more than 35 years. The model lineup includes deck boats, sport boats, sport decks, cuddies, and a mid-cabin cruiser. Stingray boasts of one of the most technologically advanced facilities in the country.

Sea Ray designs, manufactures and markets boats ranging from 17 feet (5.2 m) power cruisers to over 65 feet (20 m) yachts. Sea Ray was the first boat manufacturer to use fiberglass in its pleasure boat construction.

Sabre Yachts has been crafting sail and motor yachts since 1970 from two plants in Raymond, Maine. Sabre’s sister company, Back Cove Yachts, operates from a facility in Rockland, ME.
